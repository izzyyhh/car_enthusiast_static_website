Everybody knows that cars cause environmental pollution

To pollute our environment is not good, no matter in which way it is done. Everyone should have the will to be environmental friendly, in order to keep our planet healthy and us humans alive. Sadly, cars pollute the air by producing CO2. Furthermore, the CO2 emissions lead to problems: for exmaple global warming. To prevent those problems we have to find a sollution. One is to stop the production of engines that use gasoline or diesel as fuel. Scientists made a lot of experiments with electric engines and now they are used in automobiles such as the BMW i8, which you can see in the header. The usage of electric cars is said to be a success and certainly a step forward.

Even better than electric cars!

The problem of electric cars is the battery pack. For the production of the accumulator, precious material from mother earth is needed, which sadly is an negative side of electric cars. Because of that, scientists experimented with hydrogen. This led to the invention of hydrogen vehicles, which use hydrogen as fuel for motive power. These vehicles had a problem with the range. Indeed, the durability of the hydrogen tank was not enough for normal consumer habits. Today the range has improved and hydrogen vehicles are getting used more and more.
